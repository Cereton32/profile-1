About Me

As Interior Designer

Check Resume

In addition to my professional experience, I also thrive in the world of freelancing. Working as a freelancer allows me to explore my creativity further and collaborate closely with clients to bring their visions to life. Whether it's a small apartment renovation or a larger-scale commercial project, I enjoy the flexibility and the opportunity to work directly with clients to understand their needs and preferences. As a freelancer, I offer services including: Interior Design Consultation: Providing expert advice on space planning, color schemes, furniture selection, and decor to clients seeking to enhance their interiors. Concept Development: Creating mood boards, sketches, and 3D renderings to visualize design concepts and ensure client satisfaction before implementation. Project Management: Overseeing all aspects of the design process, from initial concept to final installation, to ensure projects are completed on time and within budget.
